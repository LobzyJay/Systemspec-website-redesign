04 Quality

Accessibility and QA are part of the system

The page should prove compliance through structure, states, and behavior, not only through descriptive text.

Pass

Must ship
  • Visible focus rings on interactive controls
  • Keyboard-safe navigation and tables
  • Reduced motion support
  • Screen-reader readable wallet states

Avoid

Anti-patterns
  • Generic gradient-heavy hero art
  • Hidden labels or color-only status
  • Hover-only meaning
  • One-off spacing and radius values
