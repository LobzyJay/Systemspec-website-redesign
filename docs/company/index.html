WHO WE ARE

STSL is the technology subsidiary of SystemSpecs Holdings — the group behind Remita, HumanManager, and Whatadeal.

We design, build, and operate the systems that move money, deliver public services, and run the schemes Nigerians rely on. Our work is mostly invisible to the people it serves. That is the standard we hold it to.

STSL sits inside SystemSpecs Holdings alongside Remita, HumanManager, and Whatadeal. Each company runs its own product and operations; we share infrastructure, leadership, and a 30-year operating standard.

TIMELINE

Three decades of Nigerian software infrastructure.

Drawn from the SystemSpecs Holdings group history; STSL is the dedicated technology arm.

  1. 1991

    Registered SystemSpecs Ltd

    The group is incorporated in Lagos.

  2. 1992

    Commenced business

    Operations begin at 241 Igbosere Street, operating as a reseller of SunSystems ERP.

  3. 1993

    Proprietary software begins

    In-house development starts with SpecPay, SpecMan, and SpecPen.

  4. 1994

    New HQ on Lewis Street

    Group moves to a Y3K headquarters on Lewis Street under a 50-year property lease.

  5. 1998

    African Agent of the Year

    Recognised as African Agent of the Year under the SunSystems partnership.

  6. 2000

    HumanManager is born

    Y2K-compliant product line ships; SpecPay, SpecMan, and SpecPen merge to form HumanManager (HM).

  7. 2002

    KPMG appointed as auditors

    Group brings on KPMG to audit the books.

  8. 2006

    Remita launches

    Remita launches as the group’s payments and collections platform; SystemSpecs is featured as a Lagos Business School case study.

  9. 2007

    Dr Christopher Kolade chairs the board

    Dr Christopher Kolade is appointed Chairman of the Board.

  10. 2010

    HumanManager Ghana

    HumanManager launches in Ghana.

  11. 2011

    HumanManager Zimbabwe

    HumanManager launches in Zimbabwe.

  12. 2013

    HumanManager Rwanda

    HumanManager launches in Rwanda.

  13. 2015

    NIBSS NAPS + Stanford SEED

    Group deploys the NIBSS Automated Payment System (NAPS); SystemSpecs is selected for Stanford SEED.

  14. 2017

    Proprietary-only + Remita Mobile

    Reseller agreement with SunSystems ends; group focuses on proprietary products and launches the Remita Mobile App.

  15. 2019

    Partnership with CyberSource

    Group enters payments partnership with CyberSource.

  16. 2020

    New HQ at Plot B22 Oniru

    Group relocates to a new headquarters at Plot B22, Oniru, Lagos.

  17. 2021

    Holdings restructure

    SystemSpecs Holdings Limited is formed, with Remita Payment Services Limited and SystemSpecs Technology Services Limited as subsidiaries.

  18. 2022

    Tier-1 PSP licence + eNaira

    Group obtains Tier-1 Payment Service Provider licence; partners with the CBN on eNaira; HumanManager Limited and SystemSpecs Geelaa Limited incorporated; Dr Ernest Ndukwe appointed Chairman.

  19. 2023

    Remita Agency Business launches

    Group launches the Remita Agency Business.
