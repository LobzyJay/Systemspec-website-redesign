FEATURES

What FundACause does.

Verified campaigns

Identity and document verification on every campaign owner before a naira moves.

Regulated payment rails

Donations clear through CBN-licensed financial institutions. Funds are not held on our ledger.

Multiple donation channels

Cards, transfers, USSD, and wallet payments. Donors give the way they already pay.

Campaign management

Updates, milestones, and donor communication built into the campaign owner dashboard.

Disbursement controls

Configurable disbursement rules, milestone-based release, and an audit trail for trustees and funders.

Institutional accounts

Multi-campaign accounts for foundations, NGOs, and corporate giving programs with role-based access.

Receipts and reporting

Donor receipts, monthly statements, and exportable reporting for audit and tax filing.

White-label for institutions

Branded campaign portals for institutions running giving in their own colours.
