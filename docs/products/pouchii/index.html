POUCHII

A wallet and service aggregation API for fintech operators.

Virtual accounts on CBN-licensed institutions, plus a consolidated API for airtime, data, utilities, bills, transfers, lending, and insurance.

Request API accessRead developer docs

WHO IT IS FOR

Operators who need rails, not a roadmap.

Pouchii is built for fintech product teams, payment service providers, cooperative wallets, and ecosystem operators who need licensed banking rails and a wide service surface without negotiating six direct integrations.

  • 01Fintech product teams shipping wallets and consumer apps
  • 02Payment service providers building merchant or biller flows
  • 03Cooperative and scheme operators needing branded wallets
  • 04Enterprise platforms embedding payments inside other workflows

FEATURES

What is in the box.

Virtual accounts

Programmable virtual accounts on CBN-licensed financial institutions, with reference, settlement, and reconciliation built in.

Bills and utilities

Airtime, data, electricity, cable, and government bills under one consolidated API.

Transfers

Inter-bank and intra-bank transfers across every Nigerian institution, with status webhooks and audit trails.

Lending rails

Origination, disbursement, and repayment endpoints for retail and SME lending products.

Insurance

Policy quoting, issuance, and claims orchestration with licensed insurance partners.

Identity

BVN, NIN, and KYC verification with documented audit trails for regulator review.

Webhooks and reporting

Signed webhooks, event replay, and a reporting layer your finance team can read directly.

Sandbox

A live sandbox with realistic test data, available before contract. Production credentials follow due diligence.
